Description

Earl Grey

From Kenya. One of the world’s most popular teas, made delicious by the addition of fragrant bergamot.
3-5 minutes recommended brew time, with water off the boil.

English Breakfast

From Kenya. A blend of premium black teas that is bold and robust, with a golden cup.
3-5 minutes recommended brew time, with water off the boil.

Jasmine

From China. Cultivated at 1500-4500 feet above sea level, this delicate green tea offers a subtle aroma of jasmine blossoms.
3-4 minutes recommended brew time, with water below boiling.

Sencha

From China. A high quality, Japanese-style green tea with a remarkably smooth finish.
3-4 minutes recommended brew time, with water below boiling.

Chai Rooibos

From South Africa. Red Rooibos garnished with ginger root, sweet cinnamon, cloves, and fancy green cardamom.
4+ minutes recommended brew time, with water off the boil.

Fancy Rooibos

From South Africa. A floral and fruity rooibos with a refined sweetness — the perfect après dinner tea.
4+ minutes recommended brew time, with water off the boil.

Chamomile

From Egypt. A classic herbal tea with soothing aromatics and a fruity finish.
4+ minutes recommended brew time, with water off the boil.

Peppermint

From the U.S.A. This herbal tea is straight up mint, with a cool, refreshing aroma.
4+ minutes recommended brew time, with water off the boil.
